About the Artist

VJ is a traditional oil painter inspired by light, stillness, and the quiet poetry of everyday life. Each painting is created with fine oils on canvas, blending impressionist influences with a contemporary sensibility.

Her works often explore themes of reflection, nostalgia, and the passing beauty of simple moments — from misty Venetian mornings to intimate still lifes.
